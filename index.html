Introducing Sparrow CRM: The Simple, Secure Alternative

Sparrow CRM isn't a service; it's a file. The entire application, along with all your data, is contained within a single `sparrow-crm.html` file on your computer. It uses your browser's engine to run, but your data never leaves the file. This provides the ultimate in privacy, portability, and peace of mind.

Get Started in 60 Seconds

Step 1: Download

Click the download link and save the single `sparrow-crm.html` file anywhere on your computer.

Step 2: Open

Open the file in your favorite web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge). No installation needed.

Step 3: Import

Drag and drop a CSV of your contacts onto the welcome screen. That's it! You're up and running.
